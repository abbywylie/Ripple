\# Informational Interview Request Email – General Template

\> This sample is designed to help students and early-career professionals request informational interviews in a professional, clear, and respectful way. You can customize this version based on your school, interests, and the person you’re reaching out to.

\---

\*\*Subject:\*\* Request for a 20-Minute Career Chat

Hi \[Recipient's Name\],

I hope you're doing well\! My name is \[Your Name\], and I'm currently a \[Year\] at \[Your University\], exploring career paths in \[Field/Industry\]. I came across your profile via \[LinkedIn/alumni platform\] and was really inspired by your experience at \[Company Name\].

If you're open to it, I’d love to briefly connect and hear more about your journey—particularly how you got started and what your role looks like day to day. I’d be grateful for 15–20 minutes of your time via Zoom or phone, whenever works best for you.

Thanks so much for considering, and I look forward to the possibility of connecting.

Best regards,    
\[Your Full Name\]    
\[University Name\], Class of \[Year\]    
Email: \[your@email.com\] | Phone: \[123‑456‑7890\]    
\[LinkedIn profile URL (optional)\]
