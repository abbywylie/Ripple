# Using Reminders

The Reminders page helps you never miss an important follow-up or meeting.

## Viewing Reminders

- See all upcoming follow-ups in one place
- Reminders are automatically generated from contact follow-up dates
- Filter by time period (7 days, 30 days, etc.)

## Follow-up Reminders

- Generated from contacts with "Next Follow-up" dates
- Shows contact name, follow-up date, and relationship details
- Helps you prioritize who to reach out to

## Meeting Reminders

- See upcoming meetings
- Get notified about meetings happening soon
- Plan your schedule effectively

## Completing Reminders

1. Click on a reminder to see contact details
2. Complete the follow-up action (send email, make call, etc.)
3. Mark as complete - the follow-up date will be updated
4. The reminder will be removed from your list

## Tips

- Check reminders daily to stay on top of follow-ups
- Use the contact's email templates for quick outreach
- Update follow-up dates after completing actions
- Set realistic follow-up dates when adding contacts
- Prioritize Tier 1 contacts for faster follow-ups

## Best Practices

- Follow up within 24-48 hours for Tier 1 contacts
- Send thank-you emails after meetings
- Keep follow-up dates realistic and achievable
- Review reminders weekly to plan your outreach
