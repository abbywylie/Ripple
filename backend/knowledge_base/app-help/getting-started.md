# Getting Started with Ripple

Ripple is a professional networking platform designed to help you build and maintain meaningful connections. Here's how to get started:

## Main Features

- **Contacts**: Manage your professional network with detailed contact information
- **Discover**: Find and connect with new people in your network
- **Meetings**: Schedule and track meetings with your contacts
- **Goals**: Set and achieve networking goals
- **Reminders**: Never miss a follow-up with automated reminders
- **Profile**: Manage your public profile and settings

## Quick Start Guide

1. **Add Your First Contact**: Click "Add Contact" on the Contacts page. Only the name is required - you can add more details later.

2. **Set Up Your Profile**: Go to Profile to add your information and make your profile discoverable.

3. **Explore Discover**: Visit the Discover page to find recommended connections or browse all profiles.

4. **Create a Goal**: Set networking goals to track your progress and stay motivated.

## Navigation

- Use the sidebar (desktop) or top menu (mobile) to navigate between pages
- The Contacts page is your main hub for managing relationships
- The Dashboard shows your overview and activity

## Tips

- Start by adding contacts you already know
- Use the follow-up settings to track when to reach out
- Set reminders to stay on top of important connections
- Make your profile public to be discoverable by others
