# Tips and Tricks for Using Ripple

Get the most out of Ripple with these helpful tips.

## General Tips

- **Start Simple**: Only name is required when adding contacts - add details later
- **Use Filters**: Search and filter to find contacts quickly
- **Set Reminders**: Use follow-up dates to never miss an important connection
- **Update Regularly**: Keep contact information current
- **Make Profile Public**: Be discoverable to grow your network

## Contact Management

- **Use Tiers**: Tier 1 for dream contacts, Tier 3 for exploratory
- **Set Categories**: Helps with follow-up timing calculations
- **Track First Meeting**: Helps calculate contact status
- **Use Search**: Quickly find contacts by name, company, or role
- **Delete Duplicates**: The app alerts you to potential duplicates

## Follow-up Strategy

- **Tier 1 Contacts**: Follow up within 1-2 days (critical connections)
- **Tier 2 Contacts**: Follow up within 2-3 days (important opportunities)
- **Tier 3 Contacts**: Follow up within 3-7 days (exploratory)
- **Auto-Calculated Dates**: Let the app suggest follow-up dates
- **Override When Needed**: Adjust dates based on your situation

## Discover Page

- **Check Recommended First**: AI-suggested matches are often most relevant
- **Use Filters**: Narrow down by role, company, or industry
- **Make Your Profile Public**: Appear in others' searches
- **Add Industry Tags**: Improves matching accuracy

## Meetings

- **Schedule Immediately**: Book follow-up meetings right after a good conversation
- **Add Notes**: Remember important details from each meeting
- **Use Calendar View**: Visual planning for your schedule
- **Link to Contacts**: Always associate meetings with contacts

## Goals

- **Break Into Steps**: Large goals are easier with smaller steps
- **Set Realistic Dates**: Achievable targets keep you motivated
- **Review Regularly**: Update progress and adjust as needed
- **Celebrate Wins**: Mark completed goals to stay motivated

## Efficiency Tips

- **Use Keyboard Navigation**: Tab through forms quickly
- **Bulk Actions**: Add multiple contacts in one session
- **Search Before Adding**: Check if contact already exists
- **Use Templates**: Email templates save time
- **Set Defaults**: Use default tier/category for similar contacts

## Best Practices

- **Follow Up Promptly**: First 48 hours are critical for Tier 1 contacts
- **Keep Notes**: Track conversation details and action items
- **Stay Organized**: Use categories and tiers consistently
- **Be Consistent**: Regular use builds a valuable network database
- **Review Weekly**: Check reminders and update follow-up dates
