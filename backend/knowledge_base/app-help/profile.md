# Managing Your Profile

Your profile page lets you manage your personal information and settings.

## Profile Information

Update your basic information:
- Name
- Email
- Company or School
- Role/Job Title
- Experience Level

## Public Profile Settings

Make your profile discoverable on the Discover page:
- Toggle "Public Profile & Discover" to make your profile visible
- Set a display name (can be different from your account name)
- Add your school/university
- Add your role
- Add industry tags (helps with matching)
- Set contact method preference (Email, LinkedIn, etc.)
- Add contact information

## Privacy

- Your public profile only shows information you choose to share
- Email and phone are never shown publicly
- You control what's visible to others
- You can make your profile private at any time

## Settings

- Update your password
- Manage notification preferences
- Control data sharing settings

## Tips

- Make your profile public to be discoverable by others
- Add industry tags for better matching in Discover
- Keep your profile updated with current information
- Use a professional display name
- Set your preferred contact method

## Profile Visibility

- **Public**: Your profile appears in Discover page searches
- **Private**: Only you can see your profile information
- Default is public, but you can change this anytime
