# Setting and Tracking Goals

The Goals page helps you set networking objectives and track your progress.

## Creating a Goal

1. Click "Create Goal" button
2. Enter a goal title (e.g., "Connect with 10 recruiters this month")
3. Set a target date
4. Add a description (optional)
5. Break it down into steps (optional)

## Goal Steps

Break large goals into smaller, actionable steps:
- Each step can be marked as complete
- Steps help you track progress incrementally
- Example: "Goal: Get 5 informational interviews" â†’ Steps: "Research companies", "Reach out to contacts", "Schedule meetings", etc.

## Tracking Progress

- View all your goals on the Goals page
- See which goals are active, completed, or overdue
- Track completion percentage for goals with steps
- Update goals as you make progress

## Goal Status

- **Active**: Goals with future target dates
- **Completed**: Goals you've finished
- **Overdue**: Goals past their target date

## Tips

- Set realistic, measurable goals
- Break large goals into smaller steps
- Review and update goals regularly
- Celebrate when you complete goals
- Use goals to stay motivated in your networking journey

## Example Goals

- "Connect with 5 people in tech this quarter"
- "Attend 3 networking events this month"
- "Schedule 2 informational interviews per week"
- "Follow up with 10 contacts this week"
- "Update my LinkedIn profile and make it public"
