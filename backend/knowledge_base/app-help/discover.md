# Using the Discover Page

The Discover page helps you find and connect with new people in your network.

## Two Tabs

### Recommended for You
- Shows contacts recommended based on your profile
- Uses AI to match you with similar professionals
- Displays similarity scores to help you prioritize
- Best for finding relevant connections quickly

### Browse All
- Shows all public profiles in the network
- Use search and filters to find specific people
- Filter by role, company, or industry
- Great for exploring the entire network

## Search and Filters

- **Search Bar**: Search by name, role, company, or industry
- **Role Filter**: Filter by job title or role
- **Company Filter**: Filter by company name
- **Industry Filter**: Filter by industry tags

## Profile Cards

Each profile shows:
- Display name
- Role and company
- Industry tags
- Contact method preference
- Similarity score (in Recommended tab)

## Making Connections

1. Browse profiles in either tab
2. Click on a profile to see more details
3. Use the contact information provided to reach out
4. Add them as a contact after connecting

## Tips

- Check the Recommended tab first for personalized suggestions
- Use filters to narrow down your search
- Make your own profile public to be discoverable
- Update your profile with industry tags for better matching
