# Using the Contacts Page

The Contacts page is your central hub for managing your professional network.

## Adding a Contact

1. Click the "Add Contact" button in the top right
2. Fill in the contact's name (required)
3. Add optional details like email, phone, company, and job title
4. Set follow-up settings (optional):
   - **Category**: Type of relationship (Professional, Personal, Academic, etc.)
   - **Tier**: Priority level (Tier 1 = Dream, Tier 2 = Great, Tier 3 = Curious)
   - **Next Follow-up**: Auto-calculated based on tier and category, or set manually
   - **First Meeting**: Date you first met this person

## Contact List View

- **Left Panel**: Compact list of all your contacts
- **Right Panel**: Detailed view of selected contact
- **Search**: Use the search bar to find contacts by name, email, company, etc.
- **Filter**: Filter contacts by company using the dropdown

## Contact Status

Contacts are automatically categorized by activity:
- **Active**: Last interaction within 7 days (green badge)
- **Awaiting Response**: 8-30 days since last interaction (yellow badge)
- **Stale**: More than 30 days since last interaction (red badge)

## Managing Contacts

- **Edit**: Click the Edit button in the detail panel
- **Delete**: Click the trash icon to delete a contact (removes all associated data)
- **View Full Details**: Click "View Full Details" to see the complete contact page

## Follow-up Settings Explained

- **Category**: Determines the type of relationship and affects follow-up timing
- **Tier**: Priority level - Tier 1 contacts get faster follow-ups (1-2 days), Tier 3 gets 3-7 days
- **Next Follow-up**: Automatically calculated when you set tier and category, but you can override it
- **First Meeting**: Helps track relationship timeline and calculate contact status

## Tips

- Hover over the help icons (?) next to follow-up settings for detailed explanations
- Use the example walkthrough when adding contacts to see how it works
- Set tier and category to get automatic follow-up date suggestions
- Regularly update contact information to keep your network organized
