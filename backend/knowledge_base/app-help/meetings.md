# Managing Meetings

The Meetings page helps you schedule and track meetings with your contacts.

## Creating a Meeting

1. Click "Add Meeting" button
2. Select the contact you're meeting with
3. Choose meeting type (Coffee Chat, Informational Interview, etc.)
4. Set date and time
5. Add location (optional)
6. Add notes or agenda items

## Meeting Views

### List View
- See all meetings in a chronological list
- Filter by: Upcoming, Today, or All
- Each meeting shows contact name, type, date, and status

### Calendar View
- Visual calendar showing all meetings
- Click on a date to see meetings for that day
- Great for planning your schedule

## Meeting Status

Meetings are automatically categorized:
- **Upcoming**: Future meetings
- **Today**: Meetings scheduled for today
- **Past**: Completed meetings

## Meeting Types

Common meeting types include:
- Coffee Chat
- Informational Interview
- Networking Event
- Job Interview
- Follow-up Meeting

## After the Meeting

1. Mark the meeting as completed
2. Send a thank-you email (use the email templates)
3. Update the contact's follow-up date
4. Add notes about what you discussed
5. Log any action items or next steps

## Tips

- Schedule follow-up meetings right after a successful meeting
- Use meeting notes to remember important details
- Set reminders for important meetings
- Link meetings to contacts for easy tracking
